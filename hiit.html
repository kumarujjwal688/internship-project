HIIT Workout Plan

Full Body

Burpees – Full-body fat-burning cardio move.

Mountain Climbers – Works core and builds endurance.

Legs

Jump Squats – Explosive movement for power and legs.

Lunge Jumps – Boost balance and leg strength.

Core

Plank Jacks – Core stability with cardio intensity.

Bicycle Crunch – Great for obliques and abdominal muscles.

Weekly HIIT Routine

  • Day 1: Full Body HIIT – Burpees, Jump Squats, Plank Jacks
  • Day 2: Core + Cardio – Mountain Climbers, Bicycle Crunch, High Knees
  • Day 3: Lower Body Blast – Lunge Jumps, Wall Sit, Jump Squats
  • Day 4: Rest or Light Walking
  • Day 5: Intensity Max – 30s Work/10s Rest, 5 rounds of 4 exercises
  • Day 6: Core & Mobility – Plank Holds, Russian Twist, Light Stretch
  • Day 7: Optional Repeat of Day 1 or Total Body Flow
