Boxing Workout Plan

Warm-Up

Jump Rope – Improves footwork, speed, and cardio endurance.

Shadow Boxing – Enhances technique, speed, and coordination.

Skill Drills

Heavy Bag – Builds power, accuracy, and punch endurance.

Speed Bag – Improves rhythm, timing, and shoulder endurance.

Conditioning

Burpees – Full-body conditioning for strength and stamina.

Mountain Climbers – Enhances explosive strength and core stability.

Weekly Boxing Routine

  • Day 1: Footwork & Technique – Shadow Boxing, Jab-Cross Practice
  • Day 2: Heavy Bag Rounds + Core Circuit
  • Day 3: Conditioning – Burpees, Jump Rope, Mountain Climbers
  • Day 4: Recovery – Light Stretching, Shadow Boxing
  • Day 5: Reaction Drills + Speed Bag Training
  • Day 6: Sparring or Pad Work + Plank Holds
  • Day 7: Mobility & Flexibility Flow
