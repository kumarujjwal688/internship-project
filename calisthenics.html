Calisthenics Workout Plan

Chest

Push-Ups – Basic but powerful chest builder using body weight.

Dips – Great for chest and triceps; use parallel bars.

Back

Pull-Ups – Strengthens upper back, biceps, and grip.

Inverted Rows – Easier pull-up variation using bar or table.

Legs

Bodyweight Squats – Build endurance and leg strength.

Lunges – Targets quads, hamstrings, and glutes.

Core

Plank – Builds endurance in abs and back.

Leg Raises – Strengthen lower abs and improve control.

Weekly Routine

  • Day 1: Full Body – Push-Ups, Pull-Ups, Squats, Plank
  • Day 2: Upper Body – Dips, Inverted Rows, Pike Push-Ups
  • Day 3: Core – Planks, Leg Raises, Hollow Body Hold
  • Day 4: Active Recovery – Light Jogging or Mobility Work
  • Day 5: Lower Body – Jump Squats, Lunges, Wall Sit
  • Day 6: Advanced Skills – Muscle-Ups, Handstand Practice
  • Day 7: Flow & Flexibility – Full Body Mobility & Stretch
