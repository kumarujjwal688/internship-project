CrossFit Workout Plan

Strength

Deadlift – Builds strength in the back, legs, and grip.

Overhead Press – Develops shoulder and upper body power.

Conditioning

Rowing – Full-body conditioning for stamina.

Box Jumps – Boosts explosiveness and cardiovascular endurance.

Functional Movements

Kettlebell Swings – Engages core, hips, and glutes.

Wall Ball – Combines squat and press for full-body training.

Weekly CrossFit Routine

  • Day 1: Deadlifts + Box Jumps + Plank
  • Day 2: Overhead Press + Rowing + Lunges
  • Day 3: Wall Balls + Kettlebell Swings + Jump Rope
  • Day 4: Active Recovery – Mobility + Foam Rolling
  • Day 5: Full-Body AMRAP – 20 mins mixed workout
  • Day 6: Partner WOD – Team strength + sprints
  • Day 7: Optional Flow or Yoga for recovery
