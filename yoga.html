Yoga Workout Plan

Chest & Shoulders

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana) – Opens the chest and strengthens shoulders.

Upward-Facing Dog – Stretches chest and improves posture.

Back

Child’s Pose – Relieves lower back tension and calms the mind.

Cat-Cow Stretch – Mobilizes spine and warms up the back.

Legs

Warrior II – Strengthens thighs, hips, and ankles.

Triangle Pose – Stretches legs, hips, and spine.

Core

Boat Pose (Navasana) – Tones abdominal muscles and strengthens balance.

Yoga Plank – Builds core and upper body strength.

Weekly Yoga Routine

  • Day 1: Chest & Shoulder Opening – Cobra, Upward Dog, Bridge
  • Day 2: Spine Health – Cat-Cow, Child’s Pose, Seated Twist
  • Day 3: Leg Strength – Warrior Series, Triangle Pose
  • Day 4: Active Recovery – Restorative Yoga, Deep Breathing
  • Day 5: Core Power – Boat Pose, Plank, Dolphin Pose
  • Day 6: Flow Session – 30-min Vinyasa Flow
  • Day 7: Flexibility & Balance – Tree Pose, Forward Bend, Pigeon
