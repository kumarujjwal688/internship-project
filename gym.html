Gym Workout Plan

Chest

Bench Press – Builds chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Cable Fly – Isolates the chest for better definition.

Back

Lat Pulldown – Strengthens lats and upper back.

Seated Row – Engages mid-back and biceps.

Legs

Leg Press – Builds strength in quads and glutes.

Leg Curl – Isolates and strengthens hamstrings.

Core

Crunches – Targets upper abs and core endurance.

Cable Crunch – Adds resistance to abdominal training.

Weekly Gym Routine

  • Day 1: Chest & Triceps – Bench Press, Cable Fly, Tricep Pushdown
  • Day 2: Back & Biceps – Lat Pulldown, Seated Row, Dumbbell Curl
  • Day 3: Legs – Leg Press, Leg Curl, Calf Raise
  • Day 4: Rest or Cardio – Treadmill, Elliptical
  • Day 5: Shoulders – Overhead Press, Lateral Raise
  • Day 6: Core & HIIT – Crunches, Plank, Cable Crunch
  • Day 7: Active Recovery – Light Stretching or Yoga
